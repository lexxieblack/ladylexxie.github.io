TooltipFlag
public record ClientTooltipFlag(boolean advanced, boolean creative, boolean shiftDown, boolean controlDown, boolean altDown) extends Record implements TooltipFlag
A version of TooltipFlag that knows about Screen and can provide modifier key states. It is patched into all vanilla uses of TooltipFlags in client classes.

When calling any tooltip method that needs a TooltipFlag yourself, use either this (by calling of(TooltipFlag)) or TooltipFlag.Default depending on the logical side you're on.
