    Fired within LevelRenderer.addMainPass(FrameGraphBuilder, Frustum, Matrix4fc, GpuBufferSlice, boolean, LevelRenderState, DeltaTracker, ProfilerFiller, ChunkSectionsToRender) after translucent "features" from entities and block entities have been rendered.
