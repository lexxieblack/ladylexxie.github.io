    • getEnchantmentLevel

      default int getEnchantmentLevel(Holder<Enchantment> enchantment)
      Gets the gameplay level of the target enchantment on this stack.

      Use in place of EnchantmentHelper.getTagEnchantmentLevel(Holder, ItemInstance) for gameplay logic.

      Use EnchantmentHelper.getEnchantmentsForCrafting(ItemStack) and EnchantmentHelper.setEnchantments(ItemStack, ItemEnchantments) when modifying the item's enchantments.

      Parameters:
      enchantment - The enchantment being checked for.
      Returns:
      The level of the enchantment, or 0 if not present.
