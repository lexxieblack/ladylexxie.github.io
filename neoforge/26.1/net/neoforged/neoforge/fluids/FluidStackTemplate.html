DataComponentGetter, TypedInstance<Fluid>, TypedInstanceExtension<Fluid>, FluidInstance, IWithData<Fluid>
public record FluidStackTemplate(Holder<Fluid> fluid, int amount, DataComponentPatch components) extends Record implements FluidInstance

Fluid variant of ItemStackTemplate.

The main difference is that fluid templates are required to have an amount, while item templates default to 1.

Most methods in this class are adopted from ItemStackTemplate.
